Privacy Policy

SlowPaperCo  ·  Last updated: May 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how SlowPaperCo ("we", "us", "our") handles information in connection with our application "SlowPaperCo Uploader" and our printable digital products business.

1. About This Application

"SlowPaperCo Uploader" is a private tool used solely by SlowPaperCo to publish our own printable PDF products (coloring books, activity books, educational worksheets) to our own social media boards and sales platforms. It is not a consumer-facing application and is not made available to the general public.

2. Information We Access

When connected to a third-party platform, the application accesses only:

  • Our own account profile (account name, board list)
  • Permission to create posts/pins on our own boards

The application does not access, collect, or process any data belonging to other users, our customers, or any third parties.

3. How Information Is Used

Access tokens obtained through OAuth are stored locally on the operator's own computer and are used exclusively to publish our own product posts. Tokens are never transmitted to any third party or external server.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share any information with third parties. The application operates entirely on a single private computer.

5. Data Retention

OAuth access tokens are stored locally and can be deleted at any time by the operator. No user data is retained on any server.

6. Customer Information (Sales Platforms)

For customers who purchase our printable products through platforms such as Etsy, Gumroad, or Amazon, all payment and personal information is handled by those platforms under their respective privacy policies. We only receive the information necessary to fulfill digital product delivery, which is automated by the platform.

7. Children's Privacy

Our products include activity books intended for children, but our products are purchased by adults. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the most recent revision.

9. Contact

For any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact SlowPaperCo at syn.nishida@gmail.com.
